# AgroConnect:

A Multilingual Smart Assistant for Farmers
- AgroConnect is a web-based multilingual smart assistant designed to address key challenges faced by farmers in rural agricultural communities, particularly Saripalli village, Visakhapatnam district. The platform bridges the gap between farmers and essential agricultural resources using modern web technologies.

**Project Overview**:

Farmers often struggle with limited access to government welfare schemes, early crop disease detection, weather updates, and agricultural inputs. AgroConnect provides a localized, user-friendly digital solution to empower farmers with timely information, tools, and community support.

**Problem Statement**:

- Farmers in Saripalli village face several challenges that affect productivity and sustainability:
- Lack of awareness about government welfare schemes
- Difficulty in early identification of crop diseases
- Limited access to quality seeds, fertilizers, and tools
- Insufficient weather updates and disaster preparedness information

**Project Objectives**:

- Enable early and accurate crop disease detection
- Provide awareness and guidance on government welfare schemes
- Offer access to essential plant materials
- Deliver real-time weather updates and alerts
- Encourage community interaction and knowledge sharing
- Promote sustainable and technology-driven farming practices

**Target Audience**:

- Farmers dependent on agriculture for livelihood
- Farmers with limited access to digital agricultural resources
- Rural suppliers and vendors
- Local agricultural officers and advisors

**Key Features**:

- Government Scheme Awareness
Updates on eligibility, benefits, and application guidance

- Crop Disease Detection
Upload crop images for early disease identification and expert suggestions

- Access to Plant Materials
Information on seeds, fertilizers, and farming tools

- Weather Updates & Alerts
Real-time forecasts, seasonal tips, and disaster alerts

- Community Knowledge Sharing
Interaction between farmers and agricultural officers


- Multilingual & User-Friendly Interface
Supports regional languages with simple navigation
